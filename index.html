My Passion

Drifting

My passion for drifting has been a journey fueled by my love for cars. Ever since I can remember, I would take every car game I owned and experiment with making the cars slide, trying to replicate that thrilling sensation. I had known about drifting as a real-life sport for a while, but it wasn't until 2021 that I truly began to pay attention. The turning point came in 2023 when I attended my first Formula Drift event at Road Atlanta, and from that moment, I was hooked. The excitement of watching skilled drivers navigate the track with precision and flair ignited a fire within me. I returned to Road Atlanta in 2024 and even had the opportunity to experience the action at Formula Drift Orlando, solidifying my love for this exhilarating sport. One day, I hope to earn my own spot on the Formula Drift roster and compete among the best in the sport.

My Sim Setup

My sim racing setup offers an incredibly realistic experience, closely mimicking the feel of driving a real car. The MOZA R9 Wheel Base delivers precise force feedback, allowing me to sense the road's texture and the car's dynamics, making each drift feel authentic. Coupled with the MOZA CRP Pedals, which provide responsive and adjustable resistance, I can truly gauge braking and acceleration, enhancing my control. The MOZA Sequential and H-pattern shifters allow for smooth gear transitions, while my custom-built handbrake gives me the ability to initiate drifts with confidence. Together, this equipment creates an immersive environment that not only sharpens my skills but also captures the thrill and excitement of real-world driving.

Design

The Design for this website was to keep it simple and clean. Colors are kept simple to avoid any weird mix of colors. Keeping a darker scheme of colors makes it easy on the eyes of the user.

A Login/Signup button has been created to allow the user to Login or Signup. In this stage of the website, an alert notifes user of a successful login or signup.
