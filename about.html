About Me

Background

My name is Drake Burke and I am on my final year at Kennesaw State University. I will be graduating in the spring with a Bachelors Degree in Cyber Security. I have always been fascinated with computers, and have seen the trouble that comes with the technology. I chose Cyber Security as my career choice because I want to make a difference in the Cyber world. I hope to one day work for a company that stops or prevents these criminals from making the cyber world an unsafe place.

I also have a deep passion for gaming, especially in the realm of sim racing and sim drifting. Ever since I first got behind a virtual wheel, I've been hooked on the thrill of competitive racing. This passion has driven me to create content that brings together fellow enthusiasts from around the world, where we can share tips, strategies, and experiences.

Experiences

I've had the incredible opportunity to attend multiple Formula Drift events, including one at my local track, Road Atlanta, and another at Orlando Speed World in Florida. These events fueled my passion for drifting and motorsports even more. Recently, I also competed in my first virtual drift competition, which was a thrilling experience as I got to put my skills to the test in a competitive setting.

This past summer, I visited Los Angeles for the first time, which was also my first-ever experience flying. While in LA, I checked off a huge bucket list item by watching my favorite baseball team, the LA Dodgers, play at Dodger Stadium—a memory I’ll never forget!

Goals

With a background in gaming and an ever-growing fascination for motorsports, I've spent countless hours fine-tuning my skills. Whether it's perfecting my drifts or chasing that perfect lap time, my goal is to bring authentic, engaging, and informative content to my audience. I strive to make my streams and videos not only entertaining but also a learning resource for everyone who shares my passion.

Looking ahead, I plan to continue growing my community, building connections with like-minded individuals, and pushing the limits of what's possible in the world of sim racing. I'm always excited to see what the future holds, and I look forward to sharing every lap of this journey with you!
